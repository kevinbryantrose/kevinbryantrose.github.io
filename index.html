Actor

Kevin Bryant Rose

Kevin Bryant Rose is a SAG-AFTRA and AEA actor based in New York City, represented by Take 3 Talent. With a career spanning network television, streaming, and the stage, Kevin brings rigorous craft and psychological depth to every role he inhabits.

His screen credits include Blue Bloods, Power Book III: Raising Kanan, FBI: Most Wanted, and a Guest-Star turn in Awkwafina is Nora from Queens on Comedy Central, alongside appearances on Netflix's Jessica Jones, Showtime's Ray Donovan, and a recurring co-star role in CBS's Bull.

On stage, Kevin has taken on some of theater's most demanding lead roles — Walter Lee Younger in A Raisin in the Sun, Lloyd Dallas in Noises Off, and Rooftop in Our Lady of 121st Street — at respected New York venues including Arc Stages, The Heights Players, and the Barrow Group Theater.

Trained in the Meisner Technique at Terry Knickerbocker Studios, the Alexander Technique at the Barrow Group, and Improvisation at Upright Citizens Brigade, Kevin's foundation is rooted in presence, specificity, and truth. He also holds a B.A. in Theater Arts from Hunter College and is completing a Master of Arts in Psychology at Harvard Extension School — a pursuit that deepens his understanding of human behavior and informs the interior life he brings to every character.

Beyond performance, Kevin is an acting and public speaking instructor, a certified standardized patient educator at leading medical institutions, and the founder of Axon Synergy LLC.
